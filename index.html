Story of Inovation


With its headquarters in Mandsour, Patna Masaale is one of the best masale manufacture of Indian. To service our consumers, we have independent distributors. Patna masale is a sister company of Gori Foods.

Gori Foods Mandsaur, founded in 1952, holds a distinguished position as both a manufacturer and wholesaler. The company's expertise lies in crafting an extensive selection of culinary products, spanning from Chili Powder to Moong Bhajiya Instant Mix, Gulab Jamun Mix, Sambar Powder, Chaat Masala, Dhaniya Powder, Turmeric Powder, and beyond. With a legacy of nearly seven decades, Gori Foods Mandsaur has established itself as a trusted name in the industry, offering quality ingredients that enhance the flavors of countless dishes.
